### Details

You can add a query to the end of this URL to return some JSON.

_type_ can be either **posts** or **spotmaps**

If you choose *posts* you may select a **maximum** of one category from this list:

Computers and internet,
Education,
Food and drink,
Grab bag,
Health and fitness,
Human relations,
Law and Government,
Media and Arts,
Religion and philosophy,
Science and nature,
Shopping,
Society and culture,
Sports, hobbies and recreation,
Technology,
Travel and transportation,
Work and money,
Writing and language.

If you choose *spotmaps* you may select a maximum of one category from this list:

Action and adventure,
Animation,
Bollywood,
Comedy,
Crime,
Documentary,
Drama,
Family,
Horror,
Music documentary,
Musical,
Period and historical,
Romance,
Science fiction and fantasy,
Short films,
Thriller,
War films,
Westerns,
World cinema.

You may choose any one tag on which to search.

### Examples

[/api?type=posts](/api?type=posts)

[/api?type=posts&category=Computers and internet](/api?type=posts&category=Computers%20and%20internet)

[/api?type=posts&tag=fox](/api?type=posts&tag=fox)

[/api?type=spotmaps](/api?type=spotmaps)

[/api?type=spotmaps&category=Science fiction and fantasy](/api?type=spotmaps&category=Science%20fiction%20and%20fantasy)

[/api?type=spotmaps&tag=clegg](/api?type=spotmaps&tag=clegg)
